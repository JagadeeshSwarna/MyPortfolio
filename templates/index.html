RTDC-(Real time data Connect)

The main aim of the project is to process huge volumes of data generated by Procter & Gamble(P&G). The main source of data is from the distributors in the form of sales, inventory, ordercuts at store level.

Python is used for data wrangling tasks and followed by data quality checks on the transformed data for any data discrepancies occurred due to wrangling techniques. Eventually based on the processed data reports are generated.

IoT based plant monitoring system

Most of the people loves to grow plants at home, but due to their hectic work schedule they find it very tough to take care of the garden The only solution to this problem is smart monitoring of the plant growth by modernizing the current traditional methods of gardening.

The system we developed will take care of the crop development with low resource utilization by providing an automatic watering system to the user. The proposed system will work based on the information send by the sensors and so growth of the plant can be estimated. RaspberryPi is used for the implementation of this system where data is stored in the cloud.

Flipkart product review analysis

In this modern world we can get everything over internet but especially in the retail sector without proper analysis we cannot purchase a product. Customers can get easily tricked by the fraudsters. So, product reviews matter a lot when it comes to E-commerce.

The project i developed will ease the process of analyzing thousands of reviews for a product. Initially for the selected product this system will scrape the reviews from the flipkart. Later, using several data wrangling techniques useful data is obtained from the reviews and presented to the user in the form of pyplot graphs.
